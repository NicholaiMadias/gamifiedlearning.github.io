Privacy Policy

How Amazing Grace Home Living collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us or submit an application, including:

  • Your name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Correspondence and messages sent through our contact form
  • Property inquiry details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Process rental applications
  • Communicate updates about available properties
  • Improve our services and website experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or as necessary to operate our services (e.g., email delivery services used to respond to your inquiries).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use browser local storage for game features and preferences on our Nexus Arcade section. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking tools.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly. We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
