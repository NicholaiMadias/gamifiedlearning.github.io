ELLA

The Relic Runner — High-Speed Historian

"History is moving at the speed of light — if we blink, we lose it. Now, can we PLEASE hurry up and save that toaster? It's pre-Collapse!"
⚡ High-Speed Historian 💛 Relic Romantic 🌀 Unfiltered Kinetic 🔔 Jingle Rig Carrier 🔍 Flash-Scanner

🪪 Core Stats

Speed95
Knowledge88
Empathy72
Reckless82
Stealth25

🪪 Core Identity

Ella is a "High-Speed Historian" — the friction between reverence for the past and impatience with the present. She doesn't just want to study history; she wants to rescue it before the world's chaos swallows it whole. Every relic she carries is a story she refuses to let die.

👓 Visual Design & Silhouette

🎒

The "Jingle" Rig

A custom tactical harness covered in small pouches and carabiners. Every mission, she attaches new "treasures." The player hears a rhythmic metallic clinking whenever she moves — the sonic signature of her obsession.

🥽

Goggles of Preservation

Large, multi-lens goggles for scanning artifacts and protecting her eyes during high-speed sprints. The lenses shift color based on the era of the relic she's examining.

🧣

The Contrast

Streamlined, high-performance athletic fabrics for mobility — juxtaposed with ancient, tattered scarves or patches from the eras she admires. The past literally woven into her future-forward kit.

🎮 Gameplay Mechanics

🔔
The Jingle Radius
Stealth / Audio

Ella's movement speed is 20% higher than other companions. But her "Jingle" generates a sound radius. The more Relics she carries, the larger it grows — alerting enemies.

✦ +20% Move Speed ⚠ Sound Radius Scales with Inventory
📡
The Flash-Scan
Exploration

When entering a new room, Ella performs a high-speed sweep. She automatically highlights "Historical Points of Interest" that other NPCs would ignore — unlocking secret lore and unique crafting materials.

✦ Auto-Detect Hidden Items ✦ Unique Lore Unlocks
💎
Sentiment-Based Inventory
Choice / Morale

Ella has her own "Personal Stash." She will occasionally refuse to carry heavy mission items if her stash is full of "Sentimental Junk." The player must decide whether to ask her to let go.

⚠ "Let Go" lowers Morale & Trust ✦ Full Stash = Higher Relic Bonuses

🏘️ Community Roles

⛏️

Scavenger-General

In "Reclamation" missions, retrieves 3× more materials — if you can keep up. Her speed makes her the most efficient resource runner in the community.

🎶

Morale Anchor

Her Jingle and energy buffer community members during tasks, turning chores into expeditions. Her enthusiasm is contagious even when her noise is a liability.

📈 Character Growth Arc

Early Game

The Collector

Obsessive, reckless, and noisy. She values objects as much as — or more than — people. Every room is a museum exhibit she's rescuing from oblivion.

Mid Game

The Reckoning

She begins to realize that her "Jingle" puts the player in danger. A mission goes wrong because of her noise. She must learn the value of silence — and sacrifice some treasures.

End Game

The Living Archive

She understands that the community IS the living history she's been trying to save. She stops looking for artifacts and starts making new ones — crafting tools, writing records, teaching.
