At Amazing Grace Home Living, we believe education and faith are not separate journeys — they are one. Zyko Learn is our vision for bringing interactive, biblically grounded learning to every resident, every neighbor, and every curious mind who walks through our doors.

What Is Zyko Learn?

Zyko Learn is an educational initiative embedded within the Seven Stars Arcade platform. Drawing from the wisdom of the Pleiades — the Seven Sisters — each module is tied to one of the Seven Star virtues: Vision (Electra), Courage (Taygete), Serenity (Alcyone), Autonomy (Maia), Sustenance (Celaeno), Patience (Sterope), and Humility (Merope).

Students and residents engage with interactive challenges, quizzes, and story-driven puzzles that make ancient truths feel alive and relevant in today's world.

"The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom." — Proverbs 9:10

Who Is Zyko Learn For?

Zyko Learn is designed for learners of all ages. Whether you are a child discovering the Bible for the first time, a teen wrestling with big questions, or an adult seeking to deepen your understanding of scripture and character, Zyko Learn meets you where you are.

Our housing residents have access to all modules at no cost. Community members and supporters can also engage via the Seven Stars Arcade at amazinggracehl.org/arcade.

Looking Ahead

We are actively developing new modules, certificates, and community challenges. Future updates will bring collaborative learning, progress badges, and faith-based skill trees — all built to encourage growth, connection, and the pursuit of a life rooted in Christ.
