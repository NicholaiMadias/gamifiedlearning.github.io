Ways to Support the Mission

  • ✉️

    Contact Us Directly

    Reach out by email to discuss partnership opportunities, donations, or volunteer involvement. We respond to every message personally.
    admin@amazinggracehl.org

  • 🙏

    Prayer Partnership

    Your prayers are the foundation of our work. We welcome prayer partners who will stand with us as we expand our housing and ministry reach across Tampa Bay.

  • 🔗

    Spread the Word

    Share our mission with friends, family, and your faith community. Every referral helps connect someone in need with stable housing and support.

  • 🏠

    Property Partnership

    If you own or manage property in the Tampa Bay area and share our mission values, reach out to explore partnership opportunities that serve our community.

  • 💛

    Financial Giving

    Your donation directly funds stable housing, faith education, and community outreach. Every gift — large or small — makes a tangible difference.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Every act of support — whether through prayer, partnership, or connection — helps someone find stability and experience the grace of God.
