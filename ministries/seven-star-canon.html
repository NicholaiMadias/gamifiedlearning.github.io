✦ The Seven-Star Canon ✦

The seven sisters of the Pleiades — each a vessel of divine virtue, each a star in His right hand.

"In his right hand he held seven stars… the mystery of the seven stars that you saw in my right hand." — Revelation 1:16, 20

Alcyone

F-type · Virtue: Serenity

Serenity that holds steady under pressure, keeping the field readable and calm.

Emerald — Step Cut

Celaeno

Neutral · Virtue: Sustenance

Sustenance that keeps the pattern alive, tending to what would otherwise wither.

Moonstone — Halo Cabochon

Electra

A-type · Virtue: Vision

Vision that sees beyond immediate gain, aligning choices with long arcs of consequence.

Sapphire — Brilliant Octagon

Maia

O-type · Virtue: Autonomy

Autonomy to choose your own pattern, resisting external pressure to rush or conform.

Amethyst — Round Brilliant

Merope

M-type · Virtue: Humility

Humility that accepts imperfection and learns from misaligned patterns.

Ruby — Heart Cut

Sterope

G-type · Virtue: Patience

Patience that waits for the right alignment instead of forcing the board.

Topaz — Princess Square

Taygete

B-type · Virtue: Courage

Courage to act when the pattern is unclear, accepting risk without recklessness.

Navy Sapphire — Marquise
